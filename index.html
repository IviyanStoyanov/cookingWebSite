Grilling Tips

  • Preheat your grill for at least 15 minutes.
  • Clean the grill grates with a wire brush before cooking.
  • Oil the grates or food lightly to prevent sticking.

Baking Tips

  • Always use room temperature ingredients for better incorporation.
  • Measure ingredients accurately using a kitchen scale for precise results.
  • Rotate baking pans halfway through baking for even cooking.

Sauteing Tips:

  • Use a high smoke point oil such as canola or vegetable oil.
  • Cook ingredients in batches to avoid overcrowding the pan.
  • Stir frequently to ensure even cooking and prevent burning.

Knife Skills Tips

  • Hold the knife with a firm grip and use a claw grip to hold the food.
  • Use a sharp knife for safer and more precise cuts.
  • Practice proper cutting techniques such as chopping, slicing, and dicing.
